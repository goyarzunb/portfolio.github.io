CNC Mill Bracket

Previous Next

What?

  • Redesign and test a mounting bracket that can be attached to a mini CNC milling machine, to accommodate a sensor.
  • Forces to be designed for:
    • X direction - 100N
    • Y direction - 100N
    • Z direction - 100N
  • Performance goals:
    • Deflection:
      • X direction - ±1mm
      • Y direction - ±1mm
      • Z direction - ±1mm
    • Weight: Reduce as much as possible from the original weight (400g).

How?

  • Calculated deflections in X, Y, and Z directions by hand.
  • Calculated both deflections and stresses in the X, Y, and Z directions with FEA.
  • Sent designs to be 3D printed and tested them.
  • Decided to keep the design that weighed less and had the least deflection when subjected to a 100 N force (in all three directions).

Results

  • The final design fulfilled the design requirements:
  • Weight: 193 gr.
  • Deflection:
    • X direction - 1.77 mm
    • Y direction - didn't test.
    • Z direction - ± 0.64 mm
