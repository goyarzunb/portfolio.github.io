What?

This project consists of a series of three posters, which aim to showcase three different things that (in my opinion, as a foreigner living in the US) are lacking in the States, and that I've loved experiencing in Denmark. The idea is to cater to American college students and incentivize visiting Copenhagen, for either vacation, doing an exchange program, etc. I am hoping that by exposing them to these things I find so amazing about Copenhagen, they will be motivated to come here and explore.

To create the media for this project, I used Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Procreate, and hand-made sketches.
