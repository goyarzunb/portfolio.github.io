Seatbelt Boxes (2022 – NU Baja SAE)

Previous Next

What?

  • Design and manufacture seatbelt boxes for off-road car.
  • Cover holes in the firewall for seatbelts.
  • Keep drivers from (possible) fire.

How?

  • Designed on SolidWorks CAD.
  • Manufacturing:
    • Water-jetted aluminum
    • Bent with sheet metal bender
  • Assembly into car:
    • Bolted into the firewall.

Results

  • The product covers holes in the firewall made for seatbelts, and keeps the driver safe from any anomaly in the powertrain, by maintaining the firewall’s purpose (protect from fire).
