Rice Spinner (Winter, 2022)

Previous Next

What?

  • Design and fabricate a device that allows our user to:
    • Wash rice without getting their hands dirty.
    • Save water in the process of washing rice.

How?

  • Used design thinking methods to brainstorm and create mock-ups.
  • Recycled a salad spinner and borrowed the spinning mechanism (crank + gears).
  • Attached a rod with food-grade silicone flaps, with a tapped stock of ABS plastic.

Results

  • The design fulfilled its purpose:
    • The user did not have to touch the rice with their hands when washing it.
    • Saved 3 cups of water per cup of rice washed.
