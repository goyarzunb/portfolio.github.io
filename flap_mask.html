Flap Mask (Winter, 2022)

Previous Next

What?

  • Design a face mask for people with high-level spinal cord injuries who use sip-and-puff straw and mouth-stick assistive devices.
  • Filter air particles for COVID-19 protection while retaining mouth access.
  • Comfortable against the skin.

How?

  • Used design thinking methods to brainstorm and create the design.
  • Created and tested mock-ups on our users.
  • Used a K94 mask, and attached two layers of ellipse-shaped neoprene rubber and food-grade silicone.

Results

  • Comfortable against facial skin.
  • Provides a tight seal around mouth devices, protecting users from COVID-19.
