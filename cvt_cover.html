CVT Cover (2023 – NU Baja SAE)

Previous Next

What?

  • Design housing for the CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission) for an off-road car.
  • Prevent injuries to drivers/mechanics by impeding interaction with CVT.

How?

  • Did research on designs from previous years - read their design papers and talked to the authors.
  • Designed on SolidWorks CAD.
  • Manufacturing process:
    • Water jet parts
    • Weld parts together
    • Sheet metal bending for Finger Protection
  • Assembly on car:
    • Bolt into chassis.

Results

  • The design fulfilled the competition rules:
  • Powertrain guarding has a 3mm thickness.
  • Leaves no space for fingers - driver/mechanics are safe.
